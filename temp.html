About Me

Welcome to my home page! I'm Yash Gosavi, a passionate programmer with a strong focus on game engine development and graphics programming.

My professional goal is to become proficient in DirectX12, Vulkan, WebGPU, and the Unreal Engine. I will be regularly updating this website with progress on my journey, including detailed blog posts and notes to share my learnings and experiences along the way.

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from India, with a deep interest in C++ development, real-time rendering, linear algebra, computer vision, and data structures and algorithms. My passion for low-level programming and computer graphics drives my continuous exploration and learning in this exciting field.

In the next 3 years (starting February 2025), I plan to build my own game engine from scratch using DirectX11 and eventually transition to DirectX12 as needed. I am also currently working on implementing a Ray Tracer, following the "Ray Tracing in One Weekend" series.

Alongside graphics programming, I have experience in Machine Learning and TensorFlow. I've been coding since I was 12 years old, and over the years, I've written literally millions of lines of code! I'm a quick learner, a dedicated worker, and have a deep passion for problem-solving and creating.

Mathematics fuels my curiosity and creativity, which I channel through my programming projects. This drive keeps me pushing forward to master new technologies and contribute to the ever-evolving world of game development and graphics.
